Your run isn’t finished until you refuel—smart post-run nutrition is part of your training. 🏃‍♂️🥤  
Drop the “30-minute window” panic: you’ve got up to 24 hours to support real muscle repair. ⏱️  
After long runs, aim for a 3:1–4:1 carb-to-protein ratio, and hit 0.5–0.9g protein per lb of bodyweight to actually rebuild. 🍚💪  
Vegan runner? Make leucine your priority—get at least 2.5g post-run, and consider mixing pea + brown rice protein for a stronger amino profile. 🌱  
Bonus recovery: tart cherry juice can cut soreness by up to 25%, and for every 1 lb you sweat out, replace 16–24 fl oz with electrolytes (not just water). 🍒⚡
