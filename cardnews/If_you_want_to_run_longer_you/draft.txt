If you want to drop your long-run PR, just running longer isn’t enough—you need to run fast sometimes 🏃‍♂️💨  
Speed Training is simple: short segments at a much faster pace than your usual runs, built to boost cardio fitness, running efficiency, and speed endurance.  
When you practice faster running, your stride and cadence clean up, and your goal pace starts to feel more comfortably controlled 🎯  
Try one of my go-to sessions: intervals (fast 1/4 mile → jog 1/8 mile), a 20–40 minute tempo, or a playful fartlek using landmarks.  
Stick to the 80/20 rule—keep 80% easy, save 20% for speed work—and you’ll improve fast without getting hurt 🔥
