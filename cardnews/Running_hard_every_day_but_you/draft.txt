Running hard every day but your PR won’t budge? Try a tempo run: slow down on purpose so you can run faster and longer 🏃‍♂️🔥  
I want you sitting right around your lactate threshold (about 86–90% max HR)—steady, controlled, and “comfortably hard.”  
Don’t force the pace faster than threshold; nail the effort and the payoff skyrockets 🎯  
Keep it classic: warm up, run 20 minutes strong (Jack Daniels’ gold standard), then cool down ⏱️  
You did it right if you finish with energy in the tank—like you could honestly go 20–30 minutes more.
