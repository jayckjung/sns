Knee or hip pain ruining your runs? Stop chasing the “longer stride = faster” myth—overstriding spikes the load on your joints and raises injury risk. 🦵⚠️  
Instead, I want you to focus on cadence: even a ~10% increase has been shown to reduce peak knee forces, helping you land closer under your body and spend less time on the ground. ⏱️🏃‍♂️  
Aim for what fits you (many runners live around 160–180 SPM), and start by nudging your step rate up just 3–5% at a time—not 10% overnight.  
Track it, tune it, and protect your runs—and your joint health.
