Your run isn’t finished until you refuel—smart post-run nutrition is part of your training, not an afterthought 🏃‍♂️⚡️  
Drop the “30-minute window” panic: you’ve got up to 24 hours to support real muscle repair.  
After long runs, hit the 3:1–4:1 carb-to-protein sweet spot—don’t go protein-only 🍌🥛  
Aim for 0.5–0.9g protein per lb of bodyweight, and if you’re plant-based, prioritize 2.5g leucine (pea + brown rice is a strong combo).  
Finish with the basics that move the needle: tart cherry for soreness, and 16–24 fl oz per lb lost with sodium + electrolytes 💧
