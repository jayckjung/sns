Don’t starve yourself to lose weight—fuel smart and let your metabolism do its job 🔥🏃‍♀️  
Before your run, use carbs as your clean-energy teammate, and on 90+ minute sessions keep topping up (30–60g simple carbs per hour) to stay strong to the finish.  
Even while cutting, I want you hitting about 1g protein per lb and enough healthy fats (around 0.5g per lb) so you stay full and recover fast 💪🥑  
After your run, skip the “reward meal” and refuel within 30–60 minutes with a light 150–300 calorie snack to dodge late-night fake hunger.  
Need an easy win? A 3:1 carb-to-protein recovery like chocolate milk nails the sweet spot 🥛🍫
