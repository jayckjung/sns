Yes, Butter Run is a real thing: seal heavy cream in a container, go for a run, and let the motion do the churning 🏃‍♀️🧈 It’s basically the classic shake-a-jar butter experiment, just remixed for runners. As you keep moving, the cream first hits a whipped stage, then the fat clumps together and separates into butter and buttermilk. If you want better odds, use heavy cream with 36%+ milkfat, leave plenty of space in the container, and know that 6+ miles tends to work better than a short run. Just keep food safety in mind too—don’t leave it out too long, and rinse the finished butter well ❄️🥛
